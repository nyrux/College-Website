Kantipur Intl College.
Welcome to Kantipur International College, established in 2012 in the vibrant city of Biratnagar. We are committed to providing a comprehensive educational experience that fosters academic excellence and personal growth. Our modern curriculum, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities empower students to thrive in their chosen fields. At Kantipur International College, we cultivate the leaders of tomorrow in a dynamic learning environment designed to prepare them for success in an ever-changing global landscape.
Our Courses

Kantipur College is committed to delivering a high-quality educational experience, empowering students to excel in their personal and professional lives.

Computer Science
The Computer Science program equips students with essential programming skills, problem-solving techniques, and knowledge of software development, preparing them for innovative careers.
Bio Science
The Bio Science course offers in-depth study of living organisms, emphasizing research and practical applications in genetics, microbiology, and ecology for future scientists.
Management
Our Management program cultivates leadership and strategic thinking skills, empowering students to navigate complex business environments and drive organizational success effectively.
HM
The Hotel Management course combines hospitality principles with practical training, preparing students to excel in various roles within the dynamic hotel and tourism industry.
