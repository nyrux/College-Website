Kantipur Intl College.
Welcome to Kantipur International College, established in 2012 in the vibrant city of Biratnagar. We are committed to providing a comprehensive educational experience that fosters academic excellence and personal growth. Our modern curriculum, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities empower students to thrive in their chosen fields. At Kantipur International College, we cultivate the leaders of tomorrow in a dynamic learning environment designed to prepare them for success in an ever-changing global landscape.
Our Courses
Kantipur College is committed to delivering a high-quality educational experience, empowering students to excel in their personal and professional lives.
+2 Science
Our program offers students the flexibility to specialize in either BioScience or Computer Science. Dive into the complexities of living systems with BioScience, or harness the power of technology and data with Computer Science. Tailor your education to align with your career goals.
+2 Management
Our management course provides a comprehensive foundation in essential business skills, featuring specialized tracks in Hotel Management, Business Studies, and Business Math. Students engage in practical learning experiences and theoretical coursework, preparing them for dynamic careers in various industries. Through a blend of critical thinking, leadership training, and analytical skills development, graduates are equipped to excel in today's competitive business environment.
